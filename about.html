About Kao

Gist

I am a CS sophomore at Amherst College with experience coding in Java and a passion for software engineering. I also like communications and working with others. Outside of tech, I am one of the musical directors of DQ, one of the school's a capella groups, and I am a DASAC dancer! I also like playing the guitar, eating/making food, and sleeping :)

My Life Story

• I was born and raised in Newark, NJ and my family is from Ibadan, Nigeria. I'm the youngest of 4 siblings and an aunt of 3 girls.

• In 2017, I went to a boarding school called Concord Academy in Concord, MA where I was in an all female a capella group called Pitches. I also acted in theatre productions every year.

• In 2019, I studied abroad in Rennes, France for a year. I took classes in French and lived with a French-Moroccan host family.

• In January 2023, I finally declared my Computer Science major.
